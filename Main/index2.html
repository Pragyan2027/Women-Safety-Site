Isha ~ SafeSteps for every Woman

In today's fast-paced world, personal safety is a paramount concern, especially for women who may find themselves in vulnerable situations. Isha is designed with these concerns in mind. This innovative application empowers women to take control of their safety, offering a suite of features that ensure help is always just a tap away. With real-time location tracking, instant SOS alerts, and emergency contacts at the ready, this app is not just a tool but a lifeline in critical moments.
