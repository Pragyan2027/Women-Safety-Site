About Our App - Isha

Our app 'Isha' is designed to empower and protect women by identifying and alerting them about unsafe areas in real time. Utilizing advanced AI and machine learning algorithms, the app analyzes crime data to pinpoint hotspots and provides users with timely notifications, safety tips, and emergency contacts. Our goal is to create a safer environment where women can feel secure and confident in their daily lives.

A woman with a voice is, by definition, a strong woman.
