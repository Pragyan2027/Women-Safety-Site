Volunteer Support

Women Empowerment Foundation

This NGO focuses on empowering women through education, vocational training, and legal aid. They provide support to women in rural areas to help them gain financial independence.

Aangan Trust

Aangan works with children in dangerous situations, including trafficking and early marriage, and helps to prevent harm to women and girls by empowering them with necessary skills.

Snehalaya

Snehalaya provides shelter, healthcare, education, and rehabilitation to women and children who are victims of human trafficking, domestic violence, or exploitation.

Sayodhya Home for Women

This NGO offers shelter to women in distress, particularly victims of domestic violence, and provides legal and emotional support to help them rebuild their lives.

Azad Foundation

Azad Foundation provides livelihood training to marginalized women, enabling them to work as professional drivers and help them transform their lives for economic independence.

Sakshi

Sakshi is dedicated to addressing violence against women and promoting gender equality. It provides legal and counseling services to women in need of safety and protection.

Majlis

Majlis provides legal advocacy and support to women facing domestic violence and sexual abuse. Their mission is to ensure women's safety through legal empowerment and justice.

SafeCity

SafeCity collects anonymous reports of sexual harassment and abuse in public spaces and works to make these spaces safer for women. They encourage community-based solutions for women's safety.

Breakthrough India

Breakthrough India runs campaigns and programs to combat gender-based violence and discrimination. They focus on changing attitudes and social norms that hinder women's safety.

Jagori

Jagori promotes women's rights and safety by raising awareness about gender-based violence. They conduct safety audits and help create safe spaces for women in urban and rural areas.
